# SecondMallow Support

**SecondMallow** helps your child develop healthy digital habits with smart screen time controls.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I set up SecondMallow?
1. Open the app and grant Screen Time (Family Controls) permission
2. Select the apps you want to limit
3. Set daily time limits or schedule downtime

### Does SecondMallow collect my data?
No. All data stays on your device. No account is required and no data is sent to any server. See our [Privacy Policy](secondmallow) for details.

### How does the reward system work?
Your child can earn extra screen time by watching a short rewarded ad.

### The app is not blocking apps correctly
Make sure Screen Time permissions are properly granted in Settings > Screen Time > Family Controls. Try toggling the blocking off and on again.
