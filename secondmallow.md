# SecondMallow Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-02-19**

SecondMallow ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency and want you to understand how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal information.** The App operates entirely on your device without requiring account registration or transmitting data to external servers.

## Data Storage
- **Blocking Lists**: Your blocked websites and apps are stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud (managed by Apple).
- **Usage Statistics**: ON-mode time tracking data (used for the GitHub-style contribution graph) is stored locally on your device.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (language, appearance, friction settings) are stored locally.

## iCloud Sync
If you use iCloud, your blocking lists may be synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud service. This is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure, and we have no access to this data.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple Screen Time API**: Used to implement blocking functionality. This is a system-level feature provided by iOS.
- **Google AdMob** (free version only): The free version displays banner advertisements. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads through iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking. For details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Apple StoreKit**: Used for in-app purchases (lifetime ad removal). Transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

Paid users (lifetime purchase) are not shown any ads, and no advertising-related data is collected for them.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
