# LocalWhisper Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-03-21**

LocalWhisper ("the App") is a voice-to-text application designed with privacy as a core principle. All audio processing happens locally on your device.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal information.** The App does not require account registration, does not transmit audio or text to external servers, and does not include any analytics or tracking.

## Data Storage
- **Audio**: Audio is recorded temporarily in memory for transcription and is never saved to disk or transmitted externally.
- **Transcription History**: Transcription results are stored locally on your device only.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (model selection, language, hotkey) are stored locally.
- **Whisper Models**: AI models are downloaded from HuggingFace on first use and cached locally on your device.

## Network Access
The App requires network access solely for downloading Whisper models from HuggingFace. No user data is ever sent over the network.

## Third-Party Services
- **WhisperKit (Core ML)**: Used for on-device speech-to-text processing. All inference runs locally via Apple's Core ML framework. No data is sent to any server.
- **HuggingFace**: Model files are downloaded from HuggingFace's public repositories. Only standard HTTP requests are made; no user data is transmitted.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
